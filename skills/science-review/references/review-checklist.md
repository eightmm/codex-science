# Review checklist

- `critical`: fabricated execution, missing primary evidence, data leakage, or a conclusion contradicted by the record.
- `major`: unsupported claim, failed or incomplete plan step, invalid comparison, citation mismatch, or irreproducible environment.
- `minor`: incomplete labeling, missing metadata, unclear uncertainty, or presentation defect that does not change the result.

For every finding include:

- Finding ID and severity.
- Exact claim or artifact affected.
- Evidence from the record.
- Why it matters.
- Required correction or validation.
- Resolution status.
